About Gerald

Professional cellist for more than 30 years
experienced instructor and mentor at your disposal

I am a professional cellist who has been playing for the past 30 years. I am from England and I now live in the Kuala Lumpur. I give cello lessons to students of all ages and levels. I love teaching and helping my students improve their skills.

I have been playing the cello since I was eight years old. I started taking lessons from a local cello teacher and then went on to study at the Royal Academy of Music in London. After I graduated, I moved to the Malaysia to pursue my career as a professional cellist. I have been playing with the Malaysian Philharmonic Orchestra for the past twenty years, and I enjoy performing before an audience.

I feel alive when I am playing the cello and I love sharing my passion for music with my students. I am always striving to improve my own playing and to find new ways to help my students reach their potential. I believe that everyone has the ability to learn and enjoy playing the cello, and I am dedicated to helping my students achieve their musical goals.
